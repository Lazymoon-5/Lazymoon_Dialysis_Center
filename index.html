Our Services

At Lazymoon Dialysis Centre, we provide high-quality dialysis care with advanced technology and a compassionate approach.

  • Hemodialysis Treatment: Regular dialysis sessions with advanced machines and experienced staff.
  • Nephrology Consultations: Expert advice and diagnosis from specialist nephrologists.
  • Diet and Nutrition Support: Customized dietary plans to support dialysis patients' health.
  • Patient Counseling: Emotional support and guidance for patients and their families.
  • Health Checkups: Regular monitoring of vitals, blood tests, and kidney function analysis.
