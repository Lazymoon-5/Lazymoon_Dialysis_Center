Homeodialysis

Homeodialysis offers patients the flexibility and convenience of receiving dialysis treatment at home. It allows for more frequent and shorter dialysis sessions, which can lead to better health outcomes and an improved quality of life. Our team provides full support and training to ensure safe and effective care in the comfort of your home.

HCV Positive Dialysis

At Lazymoon Dialysis Centre, we offer dedicated facilities for patients with Hepatitis C (HCV). Our protocols are designed to prevent cross-infection, ensuring safe and hygienic dialysis treatment. We prioritize infection control and maintain strict isolation standards, giving HCV-positive patients access to quality care without discrimination.
